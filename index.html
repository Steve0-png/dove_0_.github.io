Hornet Spear Throw!

From the Hollow Knight and Silksong collection of fanart!

This was around the time the indie game Silksong was released, and nearly everyone who ever played a video game knew about Hornet. This was one of the first pieces done of Hornet, referenced from the first boss fight against her in the original 'Hollow Knight.'

Silktober 2025, Day 6: Tool

One of Dove's entries for Silktober 2025

This one was made for day 6 of the 2025 silktober challenge, also of Hornet, but stabbing herself with the highly addictive lifeblood tool. There's more silktober entries here.
